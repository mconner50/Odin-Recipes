This cheeseburger is just going to be a simple cheeseburger that anyone can eat. a classic cheeseburger is amazing during the summer especially if its grilled and we will make a grilled cheeseburger. It will consist of a couple of ingredients list below.

Ingredients

    Ground beef Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Brioche buns
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • bacon
  • sirracha
  • mayonaise

How to make the Cheeseburger

  1. youll want to pat out your ground beef into the shape of a classic hamburger and blend some onions into it.
  2. make sure your grill is heated.
  3. place burgers on the grill and cook for 2 minutes then rotate it 90 degrees for nice look for 2 more minutes.
  4. add bacon and onions to the medium heated side of the grill and cook it.
  5. Next flip the burger and do the same process before 2 minutes then rotate 90 degrees for another 2minutes.
  6. remove the bacon from the grill if its full cooked and if not move it to a lower heated part of the grill.
  7. next we butter the brioche buns and toast them on the grill on the low heated part of the grill.
  8. next remove the buns, bacon, and onions from the grill and finely chop the bacon.
  9. if you want to make a nice sauce mix the sirracha and mayo with a little bit of salt until its the color of mixed sirracha and mayo.
  10. now assemble your cheeseburger with bacon, letteuce, tomato, onions, and the sirracha mayo sauce
